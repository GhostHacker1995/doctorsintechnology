Welcome To Doctors In Technology

Bridging Medicine, Technology & Policy Innovation

Doctors in Technology is a clinician-driven community and policy-focused organization advancing the integration of technology into healthcare systems across Africa. We bring together doctors, engineers, researchers, and innovators to design, test, and influence solutions that improve health outcomes in real-world settings.

Research & Policy Influence

We generate evidence through research and real-world implementation, translating insights into policy recommendations that shape the future of healthcare systems.

What We Do

Driving Digital Health Innovation & Policy

Doctors in Technology operates at the intersection of healthcare, technology, and policy. We design solutions, build capacity, and generate evidence to transform health systems in Africa.

Community & Network Building

We bring together doctors, engineers, and innovators to collaborate, share knowledge, and build solutions that address real healthcare challenges.

Digital Health Training

We equip healthcare professionals with skills in AI, data science, and software development to actively participate in building the future of medicine.

Innovation & Product Development

We support the design and development of digital health tools, from telemedicine platforms to AI-driven clinical solutions tailored for low-resource settings.

Research & Evidence Generation

We conduct and support research that explores the impact of technology in healthcare, generating insights that inform practice and decision-making.

Policy Development & Advisory

We translate research and real-world experience into policy recommendations, working with stakeholders to shape effective and sustainable health systems.

Strategic Partnerships

We collaborate with governments, NGOs, academia, and private sector players to scale impactful digital health solutions across Africa.

Why Work With Us

Leading the Integration of Technology into Healthcare Across Africa

Doctors in Technology unites clinicians, engineers, and researchers to develop digital health solutions that are evidence-based, scalable, and ethical. We combine real-world clinical insight with innovation to drive systemic improvements in health outcomes.

By joining our community, partners and members gain access to mentorship, training, and collaborative opportunities that shape the future of healthcare.

Community of Innovators

We connect doctors and tech experts to collaborate, exchange knowledge, and co-create impactful solutions.

Evidence-Based Research

Policy Influence & Advisory
